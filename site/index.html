Internet home of the world's best worst pixel artist and actual coniferous plant creature in real life - it's PencilVoid. Take a look around, get comfy.

Who/what/why are you?

I am a PencilVoid. As far as I know, I am the only person who is me.

This place is a personal project that I became unexpectedly invested in. I've used this to learn basic web development and computer skills in general. It's been fun so far.

It's also a place for me to talk about the things I do. Most of my hobbies involve sitting in front of an oversized calculator and pressing buttons until it shows cool stuff on its display. This website is an example of that. Some things I do with my giant calculator include pixel art and amateur video game modding, which I do with tools like Paint.NET and Blockbench. Conveniently, this site holds a list of tools I use alongside some tools I don't use but are cool nonetheless. Maybe check that out?

This place is unique compared to my other online outlets in that it's not a stream of my thoughts and current activities, but rather an archive. The beauty of a personal website is it's inherently made to stand against the test of time, compared to social media feeds which snuff your account out and turn your posts invisible if you don't feed the machine often enough. For that reason, I think personal websites are pretty valuable. I hope you find mine to be at least somewhat valuable too.
