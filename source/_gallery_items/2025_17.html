I wanted to try doing something a little lower resolution for a change, so I did a little portrait of Ocha the deer. I tried something different with this one, where I picked a few colours from her usual colour scheme and put them together into one "ramp" to use for shading. I'm not totally in love with how it turned out; it looks a bit too similar to her normal colour scheme for the effect to come across well, but I'm glad I tried it regardless. Maybe it would work better on a character with a more diverse colour scheme.
